Academia ReVanced

Privacy Policy • Last updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Academia ReVanced. This extension is designed to enhance your experience on the SRM Academia portal by automating feedback and tracking margins. We take your privacy seriously.

2. Information Collection

Academia ReVanced does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All logic is executed locally within your browser context.

  • No personal identification is gathered.
  • No browsing data is sent to external servers.
  • No third-party trackers or analytics are used.

3. Local Storage

The extension uses your browser's local storage solely to store your preferences (such as rating choices and comments). This data remains on your device and is never shared.

4. Permissions Overview

The extension requires specific permissions to function correctly:

  • storage: To remember your auto-fill settings.
  • activeTab & scripting: To inject the automation scripts required for the SRM Academia portal features.

5. Security Assurance

The extension operates as a local layer over the official SRM portal. It does not have access to your passwords or sensitive personal records beyond what is necessary to interact with the feedback forms you intentionally navigate to.
